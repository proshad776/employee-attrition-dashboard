# 👥 Employee Attrition Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Overview

The Employee Attrition Dashboard is an interactive Microsoft Excel project that analyzes employee turnover, workforce demographics, and key HR metrics. The dashboard transforms HR data into meaningful visualizations and insights, helping organizations understand attrition trends and support data-driven workforce decisions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition trends.
- Monitor workforce demographics.
- Identify factors influencing employee turnover.
- Track key HR performance indicators (KPIs).
- Support strategic HR decision-making through interactive dashboards.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Employee Overview
- Attrition Analysis
- Department-wise Attrition
- Gender Distribution
- Age Group Analysis
- Education & Job Role Analysis
- Monthly Income Analysis
- Interactive Slicers & Filters
- KPI Cards

## 📂 Dataset

The project uses an HR employee dataset containing workforce demographics, department information, education level, job roles, salary, job satisfaction, years at the company, and employee attrition status.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Employees
- Active Employees
- Employees Who Left
- Attrition Rate
- Average Age
- Average Monthly Income
- Average Years at Company
- Department-wise Attrition

---

## 🔍 Business Insights

- Identified departments with the highest employee turnover.
- Compared attrition across different age groups and genders.
- Analyzed the relationship between salary, job role, and attrition.
- Evaluated workforce demographics to support HR planning.
- Created interactive reports for faster decision-making.
